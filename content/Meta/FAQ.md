#### If I get stuck on the other side of a rift, what do I do?
Some of the wildlife drop crystals when they die. These crystals can get you back to earth when thrown at your feet. HOWEVER engaging wildlife is incredibly dangerous, and should not be attempted alone or without an escape plan. If possible, have an Awakened obtain the crystal for you, since they are much better equipped to fight wildlife.

Additionally, try to stay near the location where you fell into the Rift if it is at all realistic. Meeting up with other people is your best shot at surviving.
