LightningLux is an ex-pro gamer and videogame streamer from the Netherlands. He is noteworthy as an Awakened through his ability to record and stream events inside the Rifts.

#### Background
LightningLux started his gaming career as part of Team Synergy (SYN) for Heroes of the Storm starting in 2015. His pro-gaming career ended when Team Synergy disbanded in 2018. He started streaming in 2019 and gained popularity by streaming Blackjack, but went dark after an incident in 2021 where he lost a large amount of money. He reappeared in the middle of 2022 as a MMORPG streamer, and soon captured an audience by playing Final Fantasy XIV, WoW and TERA. He caused a surge in popularity for the older MMORPG Flyff, and he is commonly associated with this game. As of 28 February 2023, he had 500k Twitch subscribers and 90k Youtube subscribers.

#### Boon
LightningLux has obtained a boon upon Awakening that allows him to summon a camera. Using this camera he can record the events that happen inside the Rifts, and stream them to his devices.
