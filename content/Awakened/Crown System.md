The Crown System appears to be an interface through which a human from Earth can become linked to the Overworld. This leads to vastly increased power and effectiveness at combating the monsters of the Overworld.

# Awakening
The Awakening is the process that links a person to the Crown System. It may be necessary to complete a test, such as a feat of heroism, to unlock this power. It is currently unknown what exactly is required to Awaken.

People who have achieved this link with the Crown System are called "Awakened".

# Interface
The Crown System grants one access to an Interface, even outside the Portals. Through their own will, Awakened can summon and read this Interface, which contains information on their Awakened self.

## Class
A descriptor for a certain power set. Classes seem to have similar powersets.

## Race
For nearly all awakened, this is Human, but there are exceptions. 

# Titles
All currently known Awakened people hold the Vanguard title. The description of this title in the Crown System indicates that they gained increased power upon Awakening. No other Titles are known.

# Boon
Some Awakened hold an additional Boon of a magical nature. This appears separate from the powers they get from their Class or Race.
