Welcome to the Awakened wiki. This is an ongoing compilation of lore about the [[Rifts]], the [[Overworld]] and the [[Crown System]].

In short, on 1-3-2023, the Shattering happened, an event where mysterious rifts started opening up all over Earth. People have fallen through, and while some died or got grievously hurt, others have 'awakened' to magical powers. It is currently unknown how much longer the Rifts will remain, and we are dedicated to learning as much as we can about them in the meantime.

We also have a Discord for further discussion. Both this wiki and the Discord are run by volunteer contributors, so behave!! Please read the [[FAQ]] before joining!!!
